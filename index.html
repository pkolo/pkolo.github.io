DIY Coffee Roasting

Dog Bowl Heat Gun Method

Welcome!

There are many ways to roast coffee at home. This page is dedicated to the "Dog Bowl Heat Gun" method. It's a clumsy, unappealing name, but I assure you that it's a simple, low-cost way of having freshly roasted coffee on a regular basis.

What You'll Need

  • Heat Gun - A handheld, hairdryer-like tool that is capable of extremely high temperatures, available online or at most hardware stores. You will need one that can get up to at least 500° F.
  • Stainless Steel Bowl - A dog-bowl style bowl, with a steep-sloped inside is preferred, but a mixing bowl would also work.
  • Trivet - Or anything non-heat-conductive that will help the bowl stay still while stirring.
  • Stirring Utensil - A wooden spoon works well.
  • Green Coffee Beans - Of course, you'll need some beans to roast. Sweet Maria's is an excellent place to buy them.
